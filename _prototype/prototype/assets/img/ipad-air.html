iPad Air weighs less than half a kilo, and it’s 20 per cent thinner.1 But even though it’s extremely light, it has an aluminium unibody enclosure that feels solid and durable in your hand.

Photos and videos look incredibly detailed and text is razor sharp on the stunning Retina display. It features over 3.1 million pixels — a million more than an HDTV.

The powerful and power-efficient A7 chip with 64-bit architecture makes everything remarkably responsive — while still delivering up to 10 hours of battery life.2

Now you can get up to 2x faster Wi-Fi performance using 802.11n with MIMO.1 And the Wi-Fi + Cellular model supports more bands for fast connections the world over.3

iPad comes with great apps for sending email, browsing the web and making video calls. It also includes apps that let you edit photos and videos, create documents, build presentations and more. And over 475,000 apps in the App Store are made specifically for iPad.4

iOS is even easier to use with features like Control Centre, AirDrop and smarter multitasking. And because it’s designed for iPad, everything not only looks beautiful, it runs beautifully.

The perfect companions for iPad Air. They instantly wake your iPad when opened and fold into a stand for reading, viewing or typing. (Sold separately.)4

iPad mini has a stunning 7.9-inch Retina display with over 3.1 million pixels. It also comes with the A7 chip with 64-bit architecture, ultra-fast wireless, iSight and FaceTime HD cameras, powerful apps and up to 10 hours of battery life.2 Yet it still fits easily in one hand.

iPad mini has a beautiful 7.9-inch LED-backlit display, the A5 chip, ultra-fast wireless, iSight and FaceTime HD cameras, powerful apps and up to 10 hours of battery life.1 So it’s an iPad in every way, shape and slightly smaller form.

iPad 2 has a 9.7-inch LED-backlit display, front and back cameras, up to 10 hours of battery life1 and powerful apps for the things you love to do every day. And you can discover over 475,000 apps made for iPad in the App Store.4
